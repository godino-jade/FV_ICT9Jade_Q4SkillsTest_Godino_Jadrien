🥗 Emergency Water & Food


You need at least 3 gallons per day, for sanitation and consuming purposes (per person). Then, you will need food like canned goods and other non-perishable food enough to supply for several days.

🩹 First Aid Kit


These kits contain tools like sterile gauze pads, adhesive tape, safety pins, antiseptic solution, and many more. You can also find in these kits, medicine and ointments that are essential for survival.

🔦 Flashlight & Whistle


These give you light in the dark, give you vision in unfamiliar places, and it can act as a signal for help. In adddition, they can give out loud noises that grabs attention even from a distance.

👕 Extra Clothes


These are needed incase your current clothes get dirtied or rained on. It is for personal comfort and hygiene. Moreover, it can include weather-based clothing like rainjackets, gloves, protective gear, etc.
